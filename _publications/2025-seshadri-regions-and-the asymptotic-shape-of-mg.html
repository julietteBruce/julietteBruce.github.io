--- layout: publication year: 2025 title: "Seshadri Regions and the Asymptotic Shape of Multigraded Regularity" authors: - Juliette Bruce - Lauren Cranton Heller - Mahrud Sayrafi - Alexandra Seceleanu doi: arxiv: "2512.05289" status: - Submitted type: - Research journal: journal_url: tags: - Syzygies - Positivity - Toric Geometry - Multigraded pdf: https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05289 highlight: true --- We introduce the Seshadri region of a subvariety, a convex region packaging the classical Seshadri constants with respect to every line bundle simultaneously. We develop the theory of Seshadri regions as a measure of positivity along subvarieties and apply it to determine asymptotic Castelnuovo-Mumford regularity for ideal powers and symmetric powers on smooth projective toric varieties.
